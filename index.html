About

Technology is a means.
Leadership is the mission.

I've spent 15 years at the intersection of enterprise technology and business strategy — managing infrastructure across 40+ global manufacturing sites, leading teams through large-scale migrations, and partnering with executive leadership to turn IT from a cost center into a competitive advantage.

Before that, I served six years in the United States Air Force — achieving the rank of Staff Sergeant through post-9/11 operational deployments. The discipline, mission ownership, and team-first culture forged in that environment are foundational to how I lead today.

I also served four years as an elected City Councilman representing New Albany's 5th District, overseeing a $30M+ annual municipal budget and championing the digital transformation of public services. Public accountability isn't a line item on my resume. It's a way of operating.

My MBA in IT Management is the framework I apply every day. I speak the language of the boardroom and the server room — and I bridge both with clarity, ownership, and an unrelenting focus on results.
